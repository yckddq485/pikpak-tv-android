## How to Install the PikPak TV App

There are two official methods, depending on your TV setup.

### Method 1: Install via Google Play Store (Google TV / Android TV with Play Store)

If your TV or TV box runs Google TV or a standard Android TV with the Play Store available, this is the easiest route:

1. Open the Play Store on your TV
2. Search for **"PikPak"**
3. Select and download the PikPak app
4. Sign in with your existing PikPak account (or create one)

Done. This gets you the latest version automatically and handles future updates through the Play Store.

### Method 2: USB Flash Drive Installation (for TVs without Play Store)

For Android TV boxes that don't have Google Play Store access — common with budget Chinese boxes like MX Player boxes, H96, X96, etc. — the flash drive method works:

1. On your computer, go to the official PikPak TV download page at `tv.mypikpak.com`
2. Download the PikPak TV APK installation package
3. Copy the APK file to a USB flash drive
4. Insert the flash drive into your TV or TV box
5. Use your TV's file manager app to locate and open the APK
6. Follow the on-screen installation prompts (you may need to enable "install from unknown sources" in settings)
7. Sign in to your PikPak account

If you get stuck on the installation side, PikPak's official Telegram group (`@pikpak_userservice`) has active support and community tutorials for different TV box models.
